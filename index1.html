About FitFury

Where Discipline Meets Strength

FitFury is a premier combat fitness training center dedicated to helping individuals achieve their physical and mental potential through martial arts, boxing, MMA, and bodybuilding.

Founded in 2015 by former MMA champion Jake Reynolds, our facility has trained thousands of students, from complete beginners to professional fighters.

We believe in a holistic approach to fitness that combines technical skill development with strength conditioning and mental discipline.
